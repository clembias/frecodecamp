A24 Films, commonly referred to as A24, is an American independent entertainment company founded in 2012 that specializes in film and television production, as well as film distribution. A24 has developed a reputation as a powerhouse in independent film with a passionate fanbase. Its projects have also had a major influence on style in contemporary horror and arthouse films. A24 is also known for the originality and artistic style of films different from the style of films produced or released by the major film studios.
